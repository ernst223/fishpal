Welcome To FishPal!

WHO WE ARE

FishPal is an admin system designed by anglers for anglersto ease the management monitoring of all South African anglers. We provide an easy to use platform for anyone from members to controlling bodies. FishPal provides a platform for the South African Sport Anglers and Casting Confederation (SASACC) to effectively and easily manage all disciplines of angling found within South Africa.
