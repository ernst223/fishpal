Overview

The South African Sports Angling and Casting Confederation (SASACC) is the governing body and controlling body of competitive angling in South Africa. SASACC is thus custodians of the angling sport in South Africa and is affiliated to the is the SA Olympic Committee (SASCOC) as well as the international Confederation of Sport Fishing (CIPS) and the international angling federations namely FIPS-M for saltwater disciplines, FIPSed for freshwater disciplines and FIPS-Mouche for the fly-fishing discipline. SASACC is also a member of the newly established African Association of Sport Fishing (AAPS). SASACC consist out of 8 national associations and federations of which they participate in about 19 angling disciplines.
